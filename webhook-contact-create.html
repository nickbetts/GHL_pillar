Webhook: ContactCreate

This event is triggered when a new contact is created in GHL.

Function in CRM

ContactCreate is the highest-impact automation trigger in your stack. It bootstraps the lead by calculating ICP score, adding contextual tags, and creating an opportunity if intent is high.

  • Loads full contact record from GHL by contact ID.
  • Runs 12-rule ICP scoring and writes the score back to the contact.
  • Runs smart-tag rules based on revenue, persona, engagement, and sector.
  • If high-intent is newly added and no opportunity exists, auto-creates opportunity in Lead stage.
  • Opportunity is seeded with persona, org size, and lead source fields.
How it works with CRM: this creates the initial prioritization and pipeline entry point, so sales sees high-priority leads first.
