3. Meta Optimisation Governance

  • Pause rule: Pause variants under 1.0% CTR after learning window and minimum spend threshold.
  • Scale rule: Scale top-quartile CTR variants with stable CPL across two consecutive review cycles.
  • Refresh rule: Replace fatigued creatives when frequency rises and CTR declines week over week.
  • Compliance rule: All compliance-adjacent claims must use approved claims library wording.

4.2 Hook Families (Launch Set)

Operational Pain

  • If it takes three exports to answer one trustee question, something is not right.
  • Campaign reporting should not take days. If it does, your stack is the problem.
  • Monday mornings were not invented for fixing spreadsheet mismatches.

Outcome Gain

  • See your campaigns and donor movement in one place. It is a good feeling.
  • Turn all that disconnected activity into decisions you can actually act on.
  • Less time in reports. More time doing the thing your charity is actually for.

Risk Reduction

  • Give your team and your trustees data they can actually rely on.
  • Fragmented systems are a governance risk. One platform is a much easier conversation.
  • Trustee reporting that does not require a prep day beforehand.

Migration Confidence

  • Switching platforms does not have to mean a quarter of disruption.
  • Keep your campaigns running while you move across. We have done this before.
  • A practical migration plan that removes the scary bit.
