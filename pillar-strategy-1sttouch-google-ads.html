    1st Touch | Google Ads (High Intent BOFU Terms)

    Paid Interception

    1. Core Targeting and Geo-Location Parameters

    Traffic capture is bounded strictly by geography and transactional intent rules to shield the budget from global click variance.

    • Bidding Implementation: Maximise Conversions (Target CPA constraint applied post initial verification pass).
    • Geo-Targeting Restrictions: Limited strictly to localised Muslim charity demand and economic density corridors, including: London, Greater Manchester, Birmingham, Leeds, Bristol, Glasgow, Edinburgh, and Cardiff.
