Pillar GTM Playbook: Execution Chapter 3 (Trust and Review Platform Sprint)

Trust-programme execution and six-week G2, Capterra, and SourceForge sprint with badge milestone targets, KPI rules, and governance checks.

Execution Chapter 3: Trust Building & Review Platform 6-Week Sprint

Trust Acceleration Workstream

1. Trust Architecture (Multi-Layer)

  • Layer A: Third-party validation across all three platforms. G2 is the primary choice for badge volume and B2B buyer credibility. Capterra has the highest charity buyer search traffic. SourceForge adds a third trust signal with low overhead.
  • Layer B: Onsite trust modules (logos, attributed testimonials, quantified outcomes, risk-reversal blocks).
  • Layer C: Paid trust creatives (testimonial cards, trustee reporting proof, onboarding confidence).
  • Layer D: Sales proof pack (objection handling for migration, adoption, budget, lock-in, governance).

3. Golden Case Studies (Minimum 3)

We need at least 3 golden case studies before awards entries, trust assets, and sales proof are finalised.

Golden Case Study 1

Theme: Spreadsheet to CRM migration and admin reduction.

Should prove: time saved, fewer manual errors, cleaner lead handling, and faster reporting.

Golden Case Study 2

Theme: Campaign reporting and donor journey clarity.

Should prove: better attribution, stronger supporter movement visibility, and improved decision speed.

Golden Case Study 3

Theme: Governance, trust, and trustee-ready reporting.

Should prove: stronger board confidence, better audit trails, and easier proof for stakeholders.

4. G2 Badge Milestone Targets

G2 badges are earned per review period (Winter, Spring, Summer, Fall). Badge criteria are based on review volume, recency, and satisfaction score. Use these as internal milestones to drive review collection effort.

Badge Entry criteria How to use it
G2 High Performer Minimum 10 reviews with a high satisfaction score relative to category size Homepage trust block, paid social creative, sales deck
G2 Momentum Leader Fastest-growing product by review velocity in the category over the period Use in outreach, events, and LinkedIn organic posts at milestone point
G2 Leader High satisfaction and high market presence score. It is harder to hit early but it is the target Flagship trust asset. Use across all channels once earned
Capterra Shortlist Sufficient reviews and profile completeness to appear in Capterra Shortlist reports Embed badge on demo landing page and in email nurture
SourceForge Top Performer / Community Choice Review volume thresholds per review period Third trust signal for the onsite trust block. Lower priority than G2 and Capterra
