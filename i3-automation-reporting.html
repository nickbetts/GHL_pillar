6. Reporting

Builds daily and weekly operating metrics from live contact and opportunity data.

Related Webhooks

No direct webhook trigger

Trigger Mode

  • POST /api/reports?type=daily for daily report.
  • POST /api/reports?type=weekly for weekly report.
  • Optional email dispatch if email query param is provided.

Actual Daily Metrics

  • New Leads Today: contacts where createdAt date equals today.
  • High-Intent Leads (Total): contacts with tag high-intent.
  • Demos Booked Today: opportunities where updatedAt date equals today and stage = Demo Scheduled.
  • Total Pipeline Opps: total count of opportunities returned.

Actual Weekly Metrics

  • Total Contacts: count of contacts returned.
  • Total Opportunities: count of opportunities returned.
  • Pipeline Value: sum of monetaryValue where stage != Won.
  • Contacts by Source: grouped by UTM Source (fallback Direct).
  • Contacts by Persona: grouped by Persona field (fallback Unknown).
  • Demo Attendees This Week: opportunities where stage = Demo Complete.
Implementation source: api/reports.js. Queries currently use API-safe limit 100.
