1. Lead Scoring

Calculates ICP score from contact data and writes updates back to GHL.

Related Webhooks

ContactCreateContactUpdate

Exact Steps

  • Webhook event arrives at /api/webhooks with contact ID.
  • Handler fetches full contact record from GHL.
  • 12 scoring rules are evaluated across revenue, urgency, authority, behavior, and fit.
  • New ICP score is compared against current contact value.
  • If changed, contact is updated with new ICP score field.
  • Result is returned to webhook caller and logged.
Dependency: GHL_TOKEN and GHL_LOCATION_ID must be valid in Vercel env.
