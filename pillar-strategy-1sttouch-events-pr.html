Pillar GTM Playbook: Events & PR

Offline interception and media placement for the 1st-touch branch.

1st Touch | Events & PR Strategy

Physical Interception

1. The 2026 UK In-Person Event Target Map

Deploy operational resources to target locations where charity directors gather to evaluate strategic infrastructure changes:

  • Muslim Tech Fest: High-signal Muslim tech and innovation audience with charity and social-impact crossover. muslimtechfest.com
  • Muslim Charities Forum Events & Training: UK Muslim-led civil society events and practitioner forums. muslimcharitiesforum.org.uk/events
  • Chartered Institute of Fundraising Events: Core UK fundraising conferences, specialist groups, and training events. ciof.org.uk/learning-and-events-listing
  • CharityComms Events: PR, media, and digital engagement events for charity communication teams. charitycomms.org.uk/events
  • Third Sector Events: Major Muslim charity-sector conferences and awards ecosystem in the UK. thirdsector.co.uk/thirdsector-events
  • Charity Times Awards: Sector visibility opportunity for proof, partnerships, and speaking submissions. charitytimes.com/awards

1.1 Event Prioritisation Notes

  • Muslim-tech route: Prioritise Muslim Tech Fest and MCF events for faith-community trust building and ecosystem referrals.
  • Muslim charity route: Prioritise CIOF, CharityComms, and Third Sector conferences for broad Muslim charity pipeline coverage in the UK.
  • Awards route: Use Charity Times and Third Sector award programmes as authority signals in retargeting and nurture assets.

2. On-Site "Tech Stack Audit Station" Model

Replace static sales banners with practical, diagnostic engagement formats:

"Do not pitch feature lists. Set up a physical evaluation space where a charity director can map current tool fragmentation, including separate donation forms, database silos, and email platforms. Deliver a 10-minute visual audit that highlights hidden administrative cost."

3. PR Core Placement Angles

  • Editorial Hooks: Pitch data-focused operational features to trade outlets, focusing on original research around administrative hours drained by outdated tools or database drop-off rates on unoptimised checkout layouts.
  • Target Media Environments: Build a regular content footprint inside Third Sector, Civil Society, Charity Digital, UK Fundraising, and Charity Times.

4. Publication & Guest Contribution Link List

Use these direct links for outreach, contributor discussions, and sponsored research placements:

  • Third Sector (contributor guidance): thirdsector.co.uk/contributing-to-thirdsector
  • Civil Society Media (advertise/partner content): civilsociety.co.uk/news/advertise-with-us.html
  • Charity Digital (advertise with us): charitydigital.org.uk/advertise-with-us
  • UK Fundraising (editorial contact route): fundraising.co.uk/contact-us
  • Charity Times (publication home): charitytimes.com/ct/index.php
