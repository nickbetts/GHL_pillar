Pillar GTM Playbook: Retargeting and Research

Validation-layer execution covering LinkedIn retargeting, competitor interception, and Meta CAPI retargeting.

Retargeting/Research | LinkedIn Retargeting Ads

Validation Layer

1. Segment Pool Parameters

Populate LinkedIn Matched Audiences through strict user-action signals:

  • Website Performance Segment: Users tracking across pillarplatform.co.uk over a rolling 90-day window, filtered by visits to features or pricing plans.
  • Form Abandonment Segment: High-priority targets who accessed the booking flow interface but closed out before completing verification.
  • Engagement Pools: Newsletter subscribers and individuals who interacted with top-of-funnel Single Image or Document items.

Retargeting/Research | Google Ads Competitor Interception

Conquesting Safeguard

Campaign architecture control: run competitor interception as a standalone campaign family (Search | Competitor) with its own budget, bidding, and negatives. Do not merge this term set into the Core Intent campaign.

1. Strategic Interception Vectors

Deploy exact match parameters to capture buyers looking for competitor platform changes or facing migration friction:

  • The Legacy Enterprise Wave (Salesforce/NPSP Transition): Capture traffic searching for alternatives amid shifting enterprise donor cloud models and NPSP certification gaps. Bid on: "Salesforce NPSP alternatives", "Nonprofit Cloud migration cost", [Salesforce nonprofit pricing], "NPSP to Nonprofit Cloud migration".
  • The Native UK Competitor Layer (Donorfy / Beacon CRM): Position Pillar as the unified system antidote to database point-solution tool sprawl. Bid on: "Donorfy alternatives", "Beacon CRM vs", [Donorfy pricing plans], "best CRM for UK charities".
  • The Standalone Form Gate (JustGiving / Enthuse): Intercept users looking to eliminate high checkout processing commissions and fee splits. Bid on: "JustGiving alternatives", "Enthuse fees comparison", [lowest fee donation platform UK].
  • Brand Defensive Guard: Bid directly on your own name brand terms (Pillar Platform, Pillar CRM) to block direct traffic poaching from competitive platforms conquesting your pipeline keywords.

2. Specialised Comparison Landing Page Blueprint

Divert competitive queries to targeted pages structured to address operational conversion friction:

  • The Cost Fragmentation Matrix: Showcase a side-by-side total cost of ownership (TCO) table. Itemise competitor point-solution bills (CRM + independent forms + plugin connections) against Pillar’s single subscription model.
  • The Trustee Validation Asset: Embed clear, downloadable summary sheets covering technical parameters including platform data encryption levels, automated UK Gift Aid submission pipelines, and full GDPR hosting controls.

Retargeting/Research | Paid Meta Retargeting (CAPI Engine)

High-Frequency Validation

1. Conversions API (CAPI) Architecture Backend

Enforce a modern tracking setup to protect custom remarketing pools from browser cookie drops:

  • Server-to-Server Integration: Deploy Meta Conversions API (CAPI) linked directly to the database layer, matching work email hash identifiers to secure high Event Match Quality indicators.
  • Exclusion Protocol Rules: Apply live exclusions to active customer profiles and converted calendar bookings to protect daily ad spend.
  • UK Location Fee Surcharge Warning: Account for the mandatory 2% UK Location Surcharge fee parameter directly inside line-item asset models to neutralise balance errors.

2. Dynamic Andromeda Creative Concepts

Meta’s delivery engine prioritises ad variety and visual targeting over manual setting configurations. Deploy three distinct creative tracks:

Concept 1: The Tool Sprawl Breakdown Graphic

  • Visual Style: High-contrast graphic map titled "The True Price of Tool Sprawl."
  • Ad Angle: Visually chart out the cost duplication of paying for standalone tools simultaneously, positioning Pillar as the cost-effective alternative.

Concept 2: The Core Workflow Screen Capture Video

  • Visual Style: A clean, user-generated-content (UGC) styled 20-second vertical screen flow.
  • Ad Angle: Demonstrate an administrator building a clean, responsive web donation form with integrated 1-click payment tools (Apple Pay / Google Pay) that auto-sync back to CRM tables.

Concept 3: The Gift Aid Administration Nightmare

  • Visual Style: Clean carousel step-by-step display highlighting operational efficiencies.
  • Ad Angle: Break down the hours required for manual Gift Aid spreadsheet processing versus Pillar’s integrated, one-click automated HMRC claim engine.
