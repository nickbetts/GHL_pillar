Webhook: InboundMessage

This event is triggered when contacts send inbound SMS or email replies.

Function in CRM

InboundMessage translates human reply intent into CRM state changes so nurture and sales actions stay synchronized.

  • Parses message text for booking intent words like book/schedule/demo.
  • Adds demo-scheduled and removes high-intent when booking intent appears.
  • Parses STOP language to apply SMS opt-out tag.
  • Writes updated tags back to contact profile.
How it works with CRM: this prevents manual triage and ensures nurture exits occur the moment a lead replies with booking intent.
