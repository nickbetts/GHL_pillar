Attraction Infrastructure

1. Target Intent Split & Persona Alignment

Content creation structures are balanced across core and adjacent buyer psychographics defined within i3MEDIA's market landscape:

  • Small Charity Operations Managers: Drowning in spreadsheet chaos and seeking to recover administrative hours.
  • Mid-Size Charity Fundraising Directors: Looking to improve strategic donor metrics (retention, campaign attribution, lifetime value) to satisfy trustee demands.
  • Charity Digital Operations Leads: Tasked with breaking down departmental data silos and eliminating legacy systems without vendor lock-in.
  • Membership Organisation Administrators: Need renewal and supporter data continuity across web and CRM workflows.
  • New Charity Founders / Volunteer-Run Funders: Require fast deployment, governance clarity, and predictable monthly spend.
  • Faith and Community Organisations: Need compliant recurring giving and campaign experiences tailored to community-led fundraising patterns.

Tier 3.1 Implementation Rules for Every Post

  • Title tags: keep within 55-60 characters where possible, with primary keyword in the first half.
  • Meta descriptions: target 140-155 characters with a clear value statement and soft CTA.
  • Secondary keyword placement: include at least 3 secondary phrases across H2/H3 headings and body copy without stuffing.
  • FAQ eligibility rule: only deploy FAQPage schema where the same questions and answers are fully visible on the page.
  • Internal links per post: minimum of 3 links to related guides plus 1 commercial link to the core i3media guide or demo route.

3. Off-Page Authority Backlink Outreach Framework

Build topical authority through a repeatable outreach system focused on Muslim charity infrastructure in the UK and relevant editorial ecosystems.

3.1 Priority Domain Tiers (Research-Led Targeting)

  • Tier 1: Sector Media and Editorial Brands - target recurring expert contributions, data commentary, and analysis placements on Third Sector, Civil Society, Charity Digital, UK Fundraising, and Charity Times.
  • Tier 2: Membership and Sector Bodies - pursue webinar speaking, practical guides, and resource citations via NCVO training and events, CIOF learning and events, and CharityComms events.
  • Tier 3: Community and Specialist Networks - secure high-relevance links through Muslim and grassroots ecosystems, including Muslim Tech Fest and Muslim Charities Forum events.

3.2 Offer-Led Link Assets (What You Pitch)

  • Original benchmark study: annual "hours lost to fragmented charity stack" report with downloadable methodology notes.
  • Operational toolkits: migration checklists, trustee-ready ROI templates, and implementation scorecards that editors can reference.
  • Expert explainers: practical pieces on donor data governance, CRM consolidation, and digital fundraising workflows.
  • Event speaker packs: conference session abstracts and workshop outlines mapped to live sector event themes.

3.3 Weekly Outreach Cadence (Execution Rhythm)

  • Monday: shortlist 15 targets (5 Tier 1, 5 Tier 2, 5 Tier 3), confirm editor/event owner, and align angle to current topic cycle.
  • Tuesday: send first-wave pitches with one clear hook, one proof point, and one concrete asset URL.
  • Thursday: send follow-up with fresh value add (new stat, framework visual, or quote option), not a generic reminder.
  • Friday: log outcomes, update relationship status, and move accepted opportunities into a 30-day content delivery queue.

3.4 Pitch Angles That Convert

  • Risk and compliance angle: how fragmented systems increase operational and governance exposure for trustees.
  • Efficiency angle: measurable admin-time recovery from consolidating CRM, website, and donation workflows.
  • Fundraising performance angle: how cleaner data improves retention, attribution clarity, and campaign pacing decisions.

3.5 Link Quality Rules and KPI Framework

  • Quality rules: prioritise context-rich editorial links, relevant anchor text, and pages that already rank for charity technology intent.
  • Monthly KPI baseline: 8-12 new referring domains, 3-5 Tier 1 placements, and at least 2 links to each core i3media asset.
  • Pipeline metrics: outreach-to-reply rate target 20%+, reply-to-placement rate target 25%+, median placement cycle under 21 days.
  • Impact metrics: growth in non-brand impressions for Muslim charity CRM terms, assisted conversions from referral traffic, and uplift in demo starts from linked assets.

3.6 Broken-Link Reclamation Protocol

  • Discovery: audit historical charity tech guides and deprecated competitor pages for dead outbound resources.
  • Replacement mapping: pair each dead reference with a like-for-like i3MEDIA resource (same intent, fresher data).
  • Outreach message: lead with reader value and content hygiene, then include the exact replacement URL and why it is updated.
