Execution Chapter 4: Delivery Annex (Dev, Marketing, Design)

Production-Ready GTM Briefing Pack
Operating mode: this annex is the execution calendar. It shows exactly what ships, who owns it, and when each gate must pass.

1. Week 1 Timeline (Day-by-Day)

Day What happens Primary owner Gate to pass
Day 1 Go-live readiness check: domain, SSL, analytics tags, conversion events, privacy/cookie basics, form QA. Web Lead + Analytics Lead No broken route, no missing event, no unresolved launch blocker.
Day 2 Website launch: publish core pages, verify top-nav flow, landing routes, and thank-you states. Web Lead All priority pages publicly reachable and form submissions recorded.
Day 3 CRM setup: lifecycle stages, required fields, routing rules, owner assignment, SLA alerts, suppression logic. CRM Admin + Sales Ops Test lead routes correctly and owner receives alert within SLA.
Day 4 Social/trust setup: LinkedIn page, Facebook page, and free trust profiles (G2, Capterra, SourceForge) live. Social Lead + Trust Lead All profiles complete with CTA, category mapping, and first post/profile update.
Day 5 Apollo setup: ICP lists, sequence templates, sending settings, CRM sync, lead-status mapping. Outbound Lead Apollo contact sync and status updates visible in CRM.
Day 6 Launch first Muslim charity themed campaign wave and publish first content batch. Paid Lead + Content Lead Campaign live with matching landing page and working conversion events.
Day 7 End-to-end test drill: ad click to lead handoff and first-response workflow. Delivery Lead Full journey passes with no dropped lead and no tracking gap.
