Webhook: OpportunityCreate

This event is triggered when a new opportunity is created in GHL.

Function in CRM

OpportunityCreate focuses on assignment and queue hygiene. New opportunities are routed to the right owner so no lead sits unworked.

  • Checks stage and current owner assignment.
  • Loads related contact to read governance role/persona.
  • Routes to fundraising, operations, or default owner based on routing env vars.
  • Writes assigned owner back to opportunity record.
How it works with CRM: this is your SLA-protection step. It ensures lead ownership is explicit at creation time.
