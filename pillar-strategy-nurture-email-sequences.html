Pillar GTM Playbook: Inbound + Outbound Email

Relationship-layer email orchestration across inbound lead follow-up, outbound segmentation, and customer review advocacy.

Nurturing | Inbound Lead Handoff Sequence

Inbound Conversion Path

1. Trigger and Promise

Every inbound form submission should receive immediate acknowledgement and a clear next action from the team.

  • Trigger: contact form or demo form submission.
  • Email 1 (instant): thank you message with "we will give you a ring" commitment.
  • Tone: short, practical, human. No long product pitch in first response.

2. Call Attempt and No-Answer Handoff

The first phone attempt should happen fast, then move to nurture only when no live conversation is reached.

  • Call SLA: first attempt within 1 business day.
  • No-answer marker: if unreachable after attempt window, mark as No Answer - Start Nurture in CRM process state.
  • Suppression rule: if call connects and demo is booked, suppress nurture sequence immediately.

3. No-Answer Nurture to Demo (4-Email Cadence)

Step Send Window Message Goal Primary CTA
Email A 2 days after no-answer marker Reconfirm the initial ask and reduce friction for booking. Book a 15-minute demo slot
Email B +3 days Show one operational win (time saved, fewer manual handoffs). See a relevant 90-second walkthrough
Email C +4 days Address trustee/leadership reporting confidence and governance proof. Get the trustee reporting template
Email D +7 days Offer direct booking with clear availability and low commitment. Pick a time this week

Nurturing | Outbound Sequence Matrix (Type x Size)

Outbound Segmentation Path

1. Segment Framework

Outbound flows should change by both charity type and organisational size so proof and pain points are relevant.

  • Types: Faith-based / Mosque, Humanitarian & International Aid, Education & Youth, Health & Care, Community / Local Services.
  • Size bands: Small (<500k), Medium (500k-5m), Large (>5m annual income).
  • Cadence baseline: 5 touches over 18 days (Day 1, 4, 8, 13, 18).

2. Sequence Blueprint by Size

Size Pain Emphasis Proof Asset CTA
Small Team capacity and manual admin overload. Case snippet on hours saved each week. Quick fit-check call
Medium Cross-team data consistency and reporting lag. Before/after campaign reporting example. 15-minute demo with reporting lens
Large Governance controls, stakeholder confidence, system sprawl risk. Governance and integration briefing note. Stakeholder-led discovery session

3. Messaging Angle by Charity Type

Faith-based / Mosque

Prioritise donor trust continuity, recurring giving confidence, and clear Ramadan/campaign reporting.

Humanitarian & International Aid

Focus on campaign agility, urgent appeal workflows, and unified supporter movement tracking.

Education & Youth

Emphasise safeguarding-friendly data handling, volunteer and parent communication clarity, and retention journeys.

Health & Care

Frame around compliant outreach, sensitive supporter segmentation, and board-level confidence in metrics.

Community / Local Services

Lead with practical consolidation of fragmented tools and faster local campaign decision-making.

Nurturing | Customer Advocacy and Review Sequence

Post-Sale Trust Expansion

1. Start Point: First Measurable Win

Review asks start when the customer has seen a concrete outcome, not on arbitrary onboarding day counts.

  • Valid win triggers: first campaign completed, first trustee-ready report delivered, or measurable admin time reduction confirmed.
  • Owner: customer success manager sends from a named person, not a generic mailbox.
  • Policy: request honest feedback only; no incentives tied to positive reviews.

2. Four-Touch Review Ask Cadence

Touch Timing Purpose Destination
Advocacy 1 Within 24h of win Thank them, reflect their win, ask for a short review. G2 (primary)
Advocacy 2 +5 days Gentle nudge with one-click option and draft prompts. Capterra
Advocacy 3 +7 days Offer alternate review destinations for convenience. SourceForge / Trustpilot
Advocacy 4 +10 days If no public review, request a private testimonial quote. Internal testimonial capture form

3. Ops Guardrails and Success Metrics

  • Suppression: stop sequence once any platform review is completed.
  • Routing: if sentiment is negative, route to support recovery workflow first.
  • Core metrics: review completion rate by platform, median days-to-review after win, advocacy reply rate, and testimonial conversion rate.
